Student packages offer focused learning environments in which students can explore and reinforce fundamental concepts[[2]](https://www.maplesoft.com/products/maple/features/Student_Packages.aspx) — Student paketlar Clickable Math xususiyatlari bilan birga step-by-step yechim imkoniyatini taqdim etadi[[2]](https://www.maplesoft.com/products/maple/features/Student_Packages.aspx).

  • • Student[Statistics] — statistika va ehtimollar

Interaktiv Bosqichli Vizual

Paket namunalari & Maple kod

1) Student[Calculus1] - Hosila topish

Bosqichma-bosqich hosila topish:

with(Student[Calculus1]):
DerivativeTutor(x^3 + sin(x));

Aniq integrallar:

with(Student[Calculus1]):
IntTutor(x^2*cos(x), x=0..Pi);

2) Student[LinearAlgebra] - Matritsalar

Matritsalar bilan ishlash:

with(Student[LinearAlgebra]):
A := Matrix([[1,2],[3,4]]);
GaussianEliminationTutor(A);

Xos qiymatlar:

EigenvaluesTutor(A);

3) Student[Statistics] - Taqsimotlar

Interaktiv taqsimot o'rganish:

with(Student[Statistics]):
ExploreRV(Normal(mu, sigma));

Gipoteza testlari:

data := [12,15,18,16,17,14,13,16,15,17];
OneSampleTTest(data, 15, alpha=0.05);

4) MultivariateCalculus - Gradient

Ko'p o'zgaruvchili funksiyalar:

with(Student[MultivariateCalculus]):
GradientTutor(x^2 + y^2, [x,y]);

Integrallash:

MultiInt(x*y, x=0..1, y=0..2);

Mustaqil ishlar va masalalar

Boshlang'ich daraja

  1. Student[Calculus1] paketini yuklang va f(x) = x² + 3x uchun DerivativeTutor ishlatib ko'ring.
  2. Student[LinearAlgebra] da 2×2 matritsaning determinantini GaussianEliminationTutor yordamida toping.
  3. Student[Statistics] da Normal(0,1) taqsimotini ExploreRV bilan o'rganing.
  4. Har bir tutorialda qadamlarni kuzating va natijalarni yozing.

O'rta va yuqori daraja

  1. Student[Calculus1] da murakkab funksiya uchun IntTutor ishlatib, har qadamni tahlil qiling.
  2. Student[LinearAlgebra] da 3×3 matritsaning xos qiymatlarini EigenvaluesTutor orqali toping.
  3. Student[Statistics] da bir nechta gipoteza testini o'tkazing va natijalarni taqqoslang.
  4. Student[MultivariateCalculus] da GradientTutor yordamida gradient tushunchasini o'rganing.
Yechimlar va maslahatlar

1-masala: with(Student[Calculus1]): DerivativeTutor(x^2+3*x)

Tutor oynasida "Show Step" tugmasini bosib, har qadamni ko'ring.

2-masala: with(Student[LinearAlgebra]): A:=Matrix([[a,b],[c,d]]): GaussianEliminationTutor(A)

Determinant = ad - bc formulasini kuzating.

3-masala: with(Student[Statistics]): ExploreRV(Normal(0,1))

Parametrlarni o'zgartirib, PDF va CDF grafiklaridagi o'zgarishlarni kuzating.
