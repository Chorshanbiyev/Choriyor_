Mapleda 3D grafiklar bilan ishlash

Mavzu: Fazoviy tushunchalarni shakllantirish · Asosiy funksiyalar: rotate3d animate

Ushbu sahifa nazariya, Maple kod namunalari, interaktiv 3D vizualizatsiya, masalalar, yechimlar va tezkor baholash bo‘limlaridan iborat. “Ko‘rsat → Bajar → Tahlil” metodikasi asosida.

Interaktiv modulga o‘tish Kod namunalari

Nazariy asoslar

3D grafik — $z=f(x,y)$ ko‘rinishidagi sirtni fazoda tasvirlash usuli. Maple’da plot3d yordamida sirtlar chiziladi, rotate3d esa sirtni aylantirish (transformatsiya) uchun qo‘llanadi. animate 3D modelning vaqt bo‘yicha o‘zgarishini (masalan, parametr $t$ ga bog‘liq) ko‘rsatadi.

Misol sirtlar: Paraboloid $z=x^2+y^2$, sinus sathi $z=\sin x\,\cos y$, konus $z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}$.

Aylanma sathlar: Agar chiziq $y=f(x)$ bo‘lsa, $x$ o‘q atrofida aylantirilganda hosil bo‘lgan sath parametrik ko‘rinishda ifodalanadi. Maple’da aylanish effektini vizual ko‘rsatish uchun rotate3d qulay.

Dars maqsadlari

  • Maple’da 3D sirtlarni chiza olish.
  • rotate3d va animate bilan ishlash.
  • Fazoviy tahlil: sirt shakli va parametr ta’sirini baholash.

Misollar & Maple kod namunalari

1) Paraboloid

$z=x^2+y^2$ sirtini chizish:

plot3d(x^2 + y^2, x=-3..3, y=-3..3, axes=boxed, grid=[60,60]);

Aylantirish (rotate3d):

with(plots):
rotate3d(plot3d(x^2+y^2, x=-3..3, y=-3..3), Pi/6, Pi/4, Pi/12);

2) Sinus sathi

$z=\sin x \cdot \cos y$ sirtini chizish va animatsiya:

with(plots):
plot3d(sin(x)*cos(y), x=-Pi..Pi, y=-Pi..Pi, grid=[60,60]);
animate(plot3d, [sin(a*x)*cos(y), x=-Pi..Pi, y=-Pi..Pi], a=0.5..2.0);

3) Konus

$z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}$:

plot3d(sqrt(x^2 + y^2), x=-3..3, y=-3..3, grid=[60,60]);

4) Aylanma sathga misol (parametrli)

Aylanish animatsiyasi g‘oyasi:

with(plots):
animate3d( (u,v) -> [ v*cos(u), v*sin(u), v^2 ], u=0..2*Pi, v=-2..2, frames=40 );

Interaktiv modul

Quyidagi parametrlarni o‘zgartirib, real vaqt rejimida 3D sirtni ko‘ring. Bu modul Mapledagi vizual g‘oyani brauzerda takrorlaydi.

Maple g‘oyasi

with(plots):
plot3d(sin(a*x)*cos(b*y), x=-R..R, y=-R..R, grid=[60,60]);
rotate3d(% , Pi/6, Pi/4, 0);
animate(plot3d, [sin(a*t*x)*cos(b*y), x=-R..R, y=-R..R], t=0.5..2.0);

Masalalar (mustaqil ish)

Boshlang‘ich

  1. Paraboloid $z=x^2+y^2$ ni $x,y\in[-2,2]$ da chizing.
  2. Sinus sathi $z=\sin x\cos y$ uchun $a=b=1$, $x,y\in[-\pi,\pi]$ da chizing.
  3. Konus $z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}$ ni $R=3$ oraliqda chizing.

O‘rtacha & Murakkab

  1. $z=\sin(ax)\cos(by)$ da $a$ va $b$ qiymatlarini o‘zgartirib, sirtning o‘zgarishini tahlil qiling.
  2. Paraboloidni rotate3d bilan turnaqator burchaklarda aylantirib, eng yaxshi ko‘rish burchagini yozing.
  3. Sinus sathi uchun animate orqali $a$ ni vaqtga bog‘lab dinamik vizual hosil qiling.
Yechimlarga yo‘l-yo‘riq (ko‘rish uchun bosing)

1‑masala: plot3d(x^2 + y^2, x=-2..2, y=-2..2)

2‑masala: with(plots): plot3d(sin(x)*cos(y), x=-Pi..Pi, y=-Pi..Pi)

3‑masala: plot3d(sqrt(x^2+y^2), x=-3..3, y=-3..3)

4‑masala: plot3d(sin(a*x)*cos(b*y), ...) — parametrlarni o‘zgartirib grafiklarni taqqoslang.

5‑masala: with(plots): rotate3d(plot3d(...), Pi/6, Pi/4, 0)

6‑masala: animate(plot3d, [sin(a*t*x)*cos(b*y), ...], t=0.5..2.0)
